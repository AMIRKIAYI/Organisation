What we are doing

We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

Clean Water

Providing sustainable water solutions for arid communities through boreholes, water pans, and rainwater harvesting systems.

Education Support

Improving access to quality education through school construction, scholarships, and teacher training programs.

Livelihood Programs

Supporting pastoralist communities with alternative livelihoods, drought-resistant farming, and livestock management.

About our foundation

Creating Lasting Change in Northern Kenya

Hands of Change was founded to address the unique challenges faced by communities in Northern Kenya's arid and semi-arid lands. Our mission is to empower these communities through sustainable development initiatives.

Since our establishment, we've worked closely with local leaders, elders, and community members to implement programs that truly make a difference in the lives of pastoralists and other vulnerable groups.
